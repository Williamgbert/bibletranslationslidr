Understanding Translation Approaches

Thought-for-Thought (Dynamic)

Focuses on conveying the meaning and intent of the original text in contemporary language, prioritizing clarity and readability.

Word-for-Word (Formal)

Maintains close adherence to the original language structure and vocabulary, preserving the form and style of the source text.
